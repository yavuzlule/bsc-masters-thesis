DUMPLINGS  COOKING  SUGGESTIONS 

TO  BOIL: 

NO  NEED  TO  DEFROST  PLACE  DUMPLINGS  IN  BOILING  WATER. 

STIRRING  SLOWLY  TO  PREVENT  STICKING.  WHEN  WATER  COMES 

TO  A  BOIL  AGAIN.  TURN  TO  MEDIUM  HEAT  COOK  UNTIL  DUMPLINGS 

FLOAT  AND  SLIGHTLY  PLUMP  (TOTAL  COOKING  TIME  APPROX  8-12  MINUTES) 

TO:  STEAM: 

NO  NEED  TO  DEFROST  PLACE  DUMPLINGS  ON  LIGHTLY 
OILED  STRAINER  OVER  POT  OF  WATER  COVER  AND  STEAM 
FOR  10-12  MINUTES.
